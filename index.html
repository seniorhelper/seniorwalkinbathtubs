Why Seniors Choose Walk In Bathtubs

A walk in tub reduces the biggest danger point in a traditional bath: stepping over a tall tub wall. In addition, it offers stability upgrades that make bathing feel comfortable again.

Lower Step-In Height

Enter and exit more safely than standard bathtubs, especially with limited mobility.

Stable Built-In Seating

Enjoy a seated soak with better balance and less strain on joints and hips.

Slip-Resistant Flooring

Designed to help reduce slips in wet conditions and improve overall safety.

Hydrotherapy Comfort

Optional jets can help soothe joint stiffness and promote better circulation.
